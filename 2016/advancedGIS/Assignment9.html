Newark, New Jersey
Ground Water Contamination

The New Jersey Department of Environmental Protection (NJDEP) has established a
Classification Exception Area (CEA) which are defined areas where the New Jersey
Ground Water Quality Standards for specific contaminants have been exceeded.
Some of these contaminants are:


    Benzene
    Chloroform
    Pesticides
    Lead
    Arsenic
    Mercury
