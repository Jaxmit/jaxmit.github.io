Newark, New Jersey

Water Contamination and Population Density

The New Jersey Department of Environmental Protection (NJDEP) has established a Classification Exception Area (CEA) which are defined areas where the New Jersey Ground Water Quality Standards for specific contaminants have been exceeded. Some of these contaminants are:

  • Benzene
  • Chloroform
  • Pesticides
  • Lead
  • Arsenic
  • Mercury
